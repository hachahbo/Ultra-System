# 🚀 Project Brief: Direct Ordering Web App for Moroccan Restaurants

## 📋 The Core Idea

Building a **multi-tenant SaaS platform** that provides mobile-optimized web applications (accessible via browser or QR codes) for local restaurants in Morocco (starting with a pilot in Tangier).

Instead of competing blindly with **Glovo** for new customer discovery, this system acts as a **Profit-Recovery and Loyalty Loophole**, converting high-cost third-party delivery orders into high-margin direct orders.

## ⚖️ Market Analysis: The "Glovo Ecosystem"

To succeed, the system exploits specific pain points inherent to Glovo's business model.

### Glovo's System & Benefits

- **Discovery Engine:** Acts as a search engine for hungry users; provides massive "free ads" and instant traffic.
- **Seamless Logistics:** Handles the entire courier fleet, route optimization, and payment processing.

### Glovo's Fatal Flaws (Our Opportunities)

- **Brutal Commissions:** Charges restaurants a 25% to 35% commission + 20% TVA on that commission.
- **Price Inflation:** Forces restaurants to mark up app menu prices (e.g., 90 DH in-store becomes 110 DH on Glovo) to survive, passing the friction onto the consumer.
- **Data Hostage:** Conceals customer names, phone numbers, and buying habits from the restaurant.
- **Zero Loyalty:** Displays competitors right next to the restaurant's menu, diluting the brand.

### 1. The "Acquire & Convert" Pipeline

## 🛠️ Technical Roadmap & Pricing Architecture

### Product Architecture

- **Phase 1 (Web App):** Lightweight, lightning-fast, mobile-optimized direct ordering interface. Zero download friction for the user. Supports "Click & Collect" and integrates local Tangier last-mile delivery APIs (or independent courier fleets).
- **Phase 2 (Native Upsell):** Once a restaurant establishes a solid core of loyal users, cross-compile the existing codebase into native iOS/Android apps via **Expo** to unlock premium push notification features.

### Sustainable Pricing Options

- **Flat Monthly SaaS:** 300 to 500 MAD/month. (Easy justification: *“If this system saves just 10 Glovo orders a month, it pays for itself.”*)
- **Low Commission (Zero Risk):** 0 MAD/month upfront + a flat 5% per order. Feels entirely risk-free to traditional owners compared to Glovo's 30%.

# 🗺️ The "Invisible Waiter" (Smart Table Mapping & Floor Plan)

## 📋 The Core Concept

A zero-friction, in-house ordering system that eliminates order mistakes and visually maps out the physical dining room for the waitstaff. The customer doesn't need to download an app or even type in their table number—they just scan, tap, and order.

## 🛠️ How It Works (The Technical Secret)

You handle all the complexity invisibly within the QR code URLs so the customer has a flawless experience.

1. **Unique QR Code Per Table:** You generate a specific QR code for every physical table in the restaurant. The URLs contain a hidden query parameter:
    - Table 1 QR points to: `order.restaurant.ma/?table=1`
    - Table 5 QR points to: `order.restaurant.ma/?table=5`
    - Table 12 QR points to: `order.restaurant.ma/?table=12`
2. **Invisible Cart Tracking:** When the customer scans the QR code, your web application reads the `?table=` ID from the URL and silently locks it to their shopping cart.
3. **The Live Kitchen/Waitstaff Map:** Instead of a boring, hard-to-read text list of orders, the restaurant gets a tablet dashboard displaying a top-down, 2D visual map of their actual floor plan.

## 💰 The Business Value (Your Core Sales Pitch)

When selling this specific feature to a Moroccan restaurant owner, emphasize these three points:

- **Eliminates Order Mistakes Completely:** Customers are notoriously bad at typing in their table numbers if asked manually (e.g., they mistakenly type Table 2 instead of Table 12). This system completely bypasses human error. The system always knows exactly where the order came from, ensuring food goes to the right spot.
- **Massive Reduction in Labor Costs:** Waiters no longer need to spend 5 to 10 minutes standing at a table writing down orders, explaining the menu, or running back and forth to the kitchen POS. They shift entirely to being "food runners" and managing customer satisfaction. On a busy Friday night, an owner can run the entire floor with 2 or 3 waiters instead of 5, saving massive money on monthly salaries.
- **Visual Workflow for Fast Kitchens:** In a loud, hectic kitchen environment, a flashing table on a visual map is processed by the human brain instantly. It is vastly superior to reading small text on printed paper tickets, drastically speeding up service time.

# 🛑 The "Double Tax" Advantage: Customer Pricing Reality

When analyzing Glovo's model, it is crucial to understand that **the customer is penalized twice**. Not only does the restaurant inflate the menu price, but Glovo also stacks delivery and service fees at checkout. This creates massive friction for the buyer.

## 🧾 The Customer's "Glovo Receipt"

When a customer orders a meal that normally costs 100 DH in the restaurant, here is what they actually pay on Glovo:

- **Food Total:** 125.00 DH *(Inflated by 25% to cover Glovo's commission)*
- **Delivery Fee:** + 10.00 to 15.00 DH *(Depends on distance)*
- **Service/Weather Fee:** + 2.00 to 5.00 DH *(Added during peak hours or rain)*
- **Customer's Final Bill:** **~ 140.00 DH**

> **The Reality:** The customer is paying 140 DH for a meal that is only worth 100 DH.
>
